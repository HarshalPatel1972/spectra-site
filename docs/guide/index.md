# What is Spectra?

Spectra is an advanced cryptographic discovery and risk intelligence platform designed to map your codebase's entire cryptographic landscape and prepare it for the post-quantum era.

## Why Spectra?
As the world moves towards Quantum-safe cryptography (like ML-KEM and ML-DSA), finding and migrating legacy algorithms (RSA, ECC, SHA-1) is critical. Spectra automates this.
